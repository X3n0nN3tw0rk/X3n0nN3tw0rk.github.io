Where Season 2 Left the Story

Season 2 concluded after events that permanently altered the world of Jujutsu Kaisen. The Shibuya Incident was not just a large scale battle. It dismantled the existing order of jujutsu society and removed any illusion of stability.

Major losses, sealed powers, and moral fractures left the remaining sorcerers in a weakened and uncertain position. Trust is damaged. Authority is questioned. Survival is no longer about strength alone but about decisions made under pressure.

From a narrative standpoint, Season 3 cannot begin by escalating chaos immediately. The story requires a moment of recalibration. Episode 1 must acknowledge the damage done and show how the world moves forward from it.
